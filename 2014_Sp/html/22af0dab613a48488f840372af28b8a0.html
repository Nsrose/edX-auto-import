Facebook Cookies

How many times have you or your friends logged into facebook on someone else's browser? How do you know your account is safe after you log out? In 2011 it was brought to public attention that Facebook was still saving cookies on browsers with the private account information even after logging out. Since then, the issue has been addressed by Facebook. However, this is just another example of how important it is to be aware of who has access to your information.
