Raffi Krikorian Bio:

Raffi is the VP of Engineering at Twitter in charge of Twitter's Platform -- that team is in charge of Twitter's core scaleable infrastructure as well as managing the software that power's the ecosystem.

Previously, Raffi was a co-founder in WattzOn.com -- which was named by Business Week as the "Best Idea" in 2008. He was the Author of "TiVo Hacks", published by O'Reilly in 2003, and used to teach at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program.
