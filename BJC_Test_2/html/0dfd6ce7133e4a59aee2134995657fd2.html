Having watched the previous video, think about the 4 stages: modeling, animation, lighting & shading and rendering. Think about how each of the objects in the short film looked when they were modeled. Where in the video did you see two of those stage at once?

The UC Berkeley Undergraduate Graphics Group (UCBUGG) is a casual bunch of students at Berkeley who want to introduce our fellow students to the process of producing a 3D animated short film.

If you liked the previous video, you may take a look at more projects here: http://www.youtube.com/user/UCBUGG/videos
