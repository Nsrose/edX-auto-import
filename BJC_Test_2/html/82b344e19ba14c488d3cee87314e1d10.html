Check out this example of recursion infront of you

Say you are sitting in a big lecture hall or a large football stadium. You want to find out what row you are in.

You can ask the person in front of you. This repeats till the guy in the front. Then undoes the recursion and goes back (adding 1 and passing it back). By the time the number gets back to you, you'll find out what row you are in.
