Bug Dance!



Every good programmer goes through this at some point, and we think that you are ready.

You need to make a bug dance.

Load this snap project. (Or, this is the same thing without the big sound file). We've made some starter custom blocks for you, in the "Looks" palette. Definitely edit... them to see what we've done.

And, we've included a few examples of how to put them together for a dance. Try it out.

Make custom blocks to do complicated steps, or to repeat sets of steps in ways that you'll do over and over. Add inputs to your custom blocks to change your steps in interesting ways. We've included several costumes in the project that you should use in new steps—look at how we used the costumes in the custom blocks we already created.

You could import your own sounds if you would like. Go to the Sounds tab above the scripting area and drag a sound from your computer.

After you've worked on this for a bit, take a moment to look at what other students have done and chat. If you see something nice, spend some time trying to incorporate that design into your program.
